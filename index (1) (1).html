What We Build

Intelligence built
for real cities

City Layout Generation

Input a ground plan and define your requirements — schools, hospitals, parks, commercial zones — and get an optimised zoning layout built around how people actually live.

Road Optimisation

Graph-based traffic modelling runs thousands of scenarios to surface the network that cuts congestion, shortens commutes, and keeps emergency routes clear at all times.

Pipeline Engineering

Terrain-aware routing for water, sewage, and utility lines — pressure-optimised and laid out so maintenance is straightforward from day one, not an afterthought.

Green Space Planner

Park and green corridor placement driven by equity — ensuring every neighbourhood has walkable access to natural space, with measurable impact on air quality and resident wellbeing.

Smart Grid Design

Energy infrastructure that accounts for renewables, load distribution, and grid resilience from the outset — not retrofitted later when it costs three times as much.

Growth Simulation

Model population density, infrastructure stress, and urban sprawl across 10, 20, and 50-year horizons — so the decisions made today don't become tomorrow's crises.

How It Works

From blank plot
to living city

01
Upload Your Plan

Import a satellite image, hand-drawn sketch, or CAD file of your land area. Our vision system interprets boundaries, terrain, and existing structures.

02
Define Requirements

Tell Gencity 3D what you need — population targets, building types, environmental constraints, and budget boundaries. Natural language accepted.

03
Algorithm Generates Plan

Our multi-model system simultaneously optimises roads, utilities, zoning, and green space — running thousands of simulations to surface the best configurations.

04
Review & Refine

Interact with a 3D city model, adjust parameters, ask follow-up questions, and export professional-grade plans ready for engineering and approval.
